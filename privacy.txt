AiPornDirect Privacy Notice
===========================

Effective date: 6 June 2024

AiPornDirect is an informational directory for consenting adults. We do not host explicit media. This privacy notice explains what limited data we process when you visit https://aiporndirect.com/.

1. Data we collect
------------------
* Server logs: IP address, user agent, and referrer collected by our hosting provider for security and abuse prevention. Logs roll off within 30 days.
* Age verification: We store a non-identifying flag in your browser (localStorage key `aiporndirect-age-confirmed`) after you acknowledge you are 18+. No personal data is stored on our servers.
* Affiliate tracking: When you click marked sponsored links we redirect you to partners who may set their own cookies. Review their policies before signing up.

2. How we use data
------------------
* To secure the site, measure aggregate traffic, and prevent minors from entering.
* To comply with legal requests related to fraud, consent violations, or abuse reports.

3. Data sharing
---------------
* We do not sell personal data.
* Hosting and analytics vendors process logs under strict confidentiality obligations.
* Sponsored partners receive referral parameters only when you click a labelled affiliate button.

4. International transfers
---------------------------
Our infrastructure may operate in the United States or European Union. By using the site you consent to data transfers necessary to deliver the service.

5. Your rights
--------------
Email compliance@aiporndirect.com to request log deletion, report underage content, or question partner practices. We respond within 30 days.

6. Updates
----------
We may update this notice as regulations evolve. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Adults only. If you are under 18 or the legal age in your jurisdiction, leave immediately.
